I am an aspiring global educator. Having lived in three different countries and having traveled to more than 25 countries has opened my eyes to the limitless opportunities of life.

I have experience teaching English to Japanese college students through my one year studying abroad at Sophia University in Tokyo, Japan. Moreover, I have experience leading and engaging in cross-cultural communication and exchange through my time as Co-President of a student organization called the USC Exchange Student Supporters.

As an East Asian Studies major, TESOL minor, and Digital Studies minor at the University of Southern California, I strive to discover the interdisciplinary connections between all three disciplines and create new ideas and knowledge in the process.
